Privacy Policy for Tejas Schools

Last updated: March 25, 2025

Tejas Schools ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile application ("the App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for users of the App.

Information We Collect

  • Camera Access: Used solely for allowing users to upload profile pictures or for in-app features that require image input. We do not collect, store, or share any camera data without your consent.
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch data from cloud services (e.g., Firebase) and to sync user progress or data.
  • User ID and Profile Info: Basic profile info like name and email are stored to provide personalized services.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used only to:

  • Provide a personalized and functional experience in the app
  • Display your uploaded content (like your profile picture)
  • Enable features based on your user type (e.g., student, teacher, admin)

We do not share your personal data with third parties, unless required by law.

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary for functionality and compliance. You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for educational use and not targeted at children under 13 without parental consent. If we learn that personal information has been collected from a child without verification of parental consent, we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
